Minecraft Rush Duel Mod

Overview

This project aims to create a functional 1.19.4 Minecraft mod that adds Yu-Gi-Oh! Rush Duels to the game. This mod will primarily focus on multiplayer content, as this will allow players to duel against one another.

I hope that this mod will be Survival friendly, meaning players can obtain most items without the need for cheats or commands. Some developer items or incomplete items may be locked behind Creative mode or commands, however.

Yu-Gi-Oh! Rush Duel

Yu-Gi-Oh! Rush Duel is version of the Yu-Gi-Oh! card game that is designed to be a simplified version of the Official Card Game and Trading Card Game. Two anime series, Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS and Yu-Gi-Oh! GO RUSH!!, showcase Rush Duels as the exclusive way to duel within these shows. Currently, the phsyical version of Rush Duels is exclusive to Japan and Korea, but the Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS anime has aired internationally.

Rush Duel Rules

Card Types

In Rush Duels, there are three types of cards: Monsters, Spells, and Traps.

Monster Cards

Monsters come in four varieties: Normal Monsters, Effect Monsters, Maximum Monsters, and Fusion Monsters. Fusion Monsters are placed in the Extra deck and all other Monster cards are placed in the Main deck.

All monsters have an Attribute, Type, Level, ATK, and DEF. In addition, Maximum Monsters have a Maximum ATK.

Spell Cards

Spell cards come in three varities: Normal Spells, Field Spells, and Equip Spells. Spell cards can only be activated on your own turn.

Normal Spells apply their effect then get sent to the GY. Field Spells remain on the field (in the Field Zone) and their effect is applied as long as they remain on the field. Equip Spells are equipped to a monster, applying their effect to the equipped monster, and their effect is applied as long as they and the monster they are equipepd to remains on the field.

Trap Cards

Trap cards come in one variety: Normal Traps. Trap cards can be set during your turn, but activated on either players turn, starting on the turn after they are set.


Duel Field

Each player has their own field on which to place their cards, with different sections.

  • Monster Zones
  • Spell/Trap Zones
  • Field Zone
  • Extra Deck Zone
  • Graveyard
  • Main Deck Zone

Deck Restrictions

Main Deck

Consists of 40 to 60 cards. Includes Non-Fusion Monsters, Spells, and Traps.

Extra Deck

Consists of 0 to 15 cards. Includes Fusion Monsters only.

Side Deck (optional)

Consists of 0 to 15 cards. Includes any type of card. This is used to swap cards between games in a match.

Copy Count

You can include a maximum of three cards with the same name in your deck.

Legend Rule

You can include a maximum of one Legend Monster card, one Legend Spell card, and one Legend Trap card in your Main deck. Your Side deck can contain any number of Legend cards.
