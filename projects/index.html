Mesozoic Island Mesozoic Island is a Discord bot game written using JDA. Players can collect dinosaurs, train them, and use them in battle. Players can also collect eggs that hatch into dinosaurs, with some dinosaurs being exclusive to eggs. Events occasionally run featuring event-exclusive dinosaurs and items. Players can show off their skills by taking on dangerous dungeons or raid bosses.
